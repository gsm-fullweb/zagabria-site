# Visual Direction: Barbearia Zagabria

## 1. Color Palette
- **Primary Black:** `#111111` (Matte Black, for a premium industrial base)
- **Gold Accent:** `#D4AF37` (Metallic Gold, for buttons, icons, and logo highlights)
- **Steel Grey:** `#333333` (Deep grey for sections and secondary backgrounds)
- **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF` (For high-contrast typography and clean lines)

## 2. Typography
- **Heading Font:** `Playfair Display` (Serif) - Transmits authority, tradition, and elegance.
- **Body/CTA Font:** `Montserrat` (Sans-serif) - Modern, readable, and perfectly suited for the industrial aesthetic.

## 3. Image Prompts
- **Hero Section:** "Premium industrial barbershop wide shot, black concrete texture walls, gold neon signs, vintage leather chairs, moody cinematic lighting with golden highlights, ultra-realistic, 8k."
- **Services Section:** "Macro shot of gold-plated barbering tools (shears and clippers) on a dark textured surface, professional lighting, focus on precision and craftsmanship, industrial luxury aesthetic."

## 4. Copy Analysis (Summary)
Based on the investigation, the copywriting focuses on "Tradition and Detail" and the "Zagabria Experience." The visual identity reinforces this by using high-contrast black and gold, which elevates the perception of value and mirrors the physical environment of the barbershop.
